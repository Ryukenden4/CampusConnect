Welcome to College InfoCenter

The College InfoCenter was developed to ease the college management for key-in and submitting data into the system. Before, the process of collecting data was still manually by Unit Residensi dan Hospitaliti Pelajar (URHP). The organization that handles UiTM Kota Kinabalu college is Unit Residensi dan Hospitaliti Pelajar (URHP) together with Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Kolej (JPK) and other departments that are involved with facilities and campus surroundings.

Prior to the implementation of College InfoCenter, the process of managing UiTM Kota Kinabalu's student housing data was manual and time-consuming. This involved URHP staff manually collecting data from various sources, such as student applications, housing assignments, and maintenance records. This manual process was prone to errors and delays, and it made it difficult to track the status of student housing applications and maintenance requests.
